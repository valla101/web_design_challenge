Temperature based on Latitude of City

This Image represents how Latitude affects the temperature of cities.


The purpose of plotting this graph was to examine the data and figure out if there is a correlation between the latitude and the temperature of a city. This plot shows that there is a correlation. When we look for correlation, visually, we want a line with a positive or negative slope. In this case the points in the data are scattered with a clear pattern. In conclusion we are able to predict whether or not a city will be hot or cold, depending on where they are located. It must be noted though that this is not something that can be predicted with absolute certainty
